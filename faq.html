Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Magic Card?
The Magic Card is a crypto-based gaming platform that offers various card games and other exciting gambling experiences. Our platform combines the thrill of traditional card games with the innovative world of cryptocurrencies.
How do I start playing?
To start playing, you need to connect your crypto wallet to our platform. Once connected, you can deposit funds and choose from our selection of games. If you're new to crypto, you can start with our free crypto faucet to get a feel for the platform.
What cryptocurrencies do you accept?
We currently accept popular cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and Binance Coin (BNB). We're constantly working on expanding our list of accepted cryptocurrencies.
Is it safe to play on The Magic Card?
Yes, we prioritize the security of our players. We use advanced encryption technologies to protect your data and transactions. Additionally, our smart contracts are audited by reputable third-party security firms to ensure fairness and transparency.
What is the "50x Potential" mentioned in your games?
The "50x Potential" refers to the maximum multiplier you can win in our games. This means you have the chance to multiply your stake by up to 50 times, depending on the game and your luck!
How does the Crypto Faucet work?
Our Crypto Faucet is a feature that allows new users to claim small amounts of cryptocurrency for free. This helps you get started on our platform without having to make an initial deposit. The faucet has a cooldown period, typically 24 hours, before you can claim again.
Can I play on mobile devices?
Yes, The Magic Card is fully optimized for mobile play. You can enjoy our games on any device with a modern web browser, including smartphones and tablets.
How do I withdraw my winnings?
To withdraw your winnings, navigate to your account dashboard and select the 'Withdraw' option. Choose the cryptocurrency you want to withdraw and enter the amount. Make sure to double-check the withdrawal address before confirming the transaction.
Are there any fees for playing or withdrawing?
We do not charge any fees for playing our games. However, when withdrawing funds, you will need to pay the standard network transaction fees for the cryptocurrency you're withdrawing. These fees go directly to the blockchain network, not to us.
What should I do if I encounter a problem while playing?
If you encounter any issues while playing, please contact our customer support team immediately. You can reach us through the 'Support' section on our website or via email at support@themagiccard.crypto. Our team is available 24/7 to assist you.
Do you have a loyalty program?
Yes, we have a loyalty program that rewards our regular players. As you play, you'll earn loyalty points which can be exchanged for bonuses, free spins, or even exclusive NFTs. Check your account dashboard to see your current loyalty level and available rewards.
Is The Magic Card available worldwide?
While we aim to be accessible globally, there are certain jurisdictions where online gambling is restricted. It's your responsibility to ensure that you're allowed to participate in online gambling activities from your location. Please check your local laws and regulations before playing.
