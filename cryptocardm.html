How to Play & Win

  1. Select any number of available crypto cards from the grid.
  2. Each card costs 0.01 BNB. The total cost will be displayed above.
  3. Click the "Buy Selected Cards" button to purchase your chosen cards.
  4. Once purchased, cards are marked as sold and cannot be bought again until the end.
  5. The game continues until all cards are sold.
  6. When all cards are sold a random player win the jackpot !
